About the club

The Web Dev Club is a student-run, tutorial-style club dedicated to teaching interested students leading-edge web technologies, fundamental to advanced. Each Web Dev Club director is an experienced developer. No prior experience with web development is required to join the club, and you do not have to be a CIS major. A basic understanding of front-end development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) will maximize your benefit from club presentations and activities. Ultimately, the club intends to make each member a full stack developer.
